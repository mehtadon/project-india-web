Most civic initiatives wait until they have a perfect plan before going public. We're doing the opposite.

## Why?

Because perfect plans don't survive contact with reality. The best ideas come from messy conversations between people who care.

When we share an idea publicly — even if it's incomplete — we invite feedback, criticism, and improvement from people with lived experience.

## The Risk

Yes, people might disagree. Yes, some ideas won't work. But the alternative — staying silent until everything is figured out — means nothing ever gets started.

## Our Approach

1. Share the idea, even if rough
2. Invite discussion openly
3. Iterate based on feedback
4. Credit the community, not individuals

This is not about being right. It's about getting better, together.
